About Me

Hello! I'm Niejhay Lazaro, an IT student with a passion for learning and creating. I’ve always been fascinated by how technology works and how we can use it to solve real-world problems — that curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Information Technology.

When I’m not coding or working on school projects, you’ll usually find me diving into online games, especially strategy-based ones. There’s something about outsmarting opponents, managing resources, and planning every move that keeps me hooked. Games like these teach patience, logic, and how to adapt — lessons I find valuable even outside the screen.

I also enjoy music. I often listen to lo-fi beats while studying or just to relax. Every now and then, I pick up my guitar and play a few chords — nothing fancy, just enough to soothe the mind. It’s one of those quiet hobbies that brings peace amidst the chaos.

One thing I really value is listening to other people’s stories. Whether it's about their struggles or achievements, I believe every person has something worth sharing. I once heard a friend talk about how he failed multiple times before finally succeeding in his career. That stuck with me. It reminded me that failure isn’t the end — sometimes, it’s just the beginning of something better.

So that’s a little bit about me — a tech enthusiast, a gamer, a music lover, and someone who’s always curious about the lives and lessons of others.
